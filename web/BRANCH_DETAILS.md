# PRASAD FOOD DIVINE - Complete Outlet Details

## About Prasad Food Divine

**Founded:** 1996 (Started with Chinese Fast Food cart at Tisgaon Naka, Kalyan)  
**First Restaurant:** 1998  
**Founder:** Mr. Nilesh Suryavanshi  
**Cuisine Type:** 100% Pure Vegetarian / Jain  
**Instagram:** [@prasadgroup](https://www.instagram.com/prasadgroup/)  
**App:** Available on App Store & Google Play Store

### Brand Philosophy

_"Prasad"_ means a gracious gift - food first offered to deity and distributed as blessing. The brand views customers as avatars of God and serves with this devotion.

### Cuisines Offered

- Punjabi / North Indian
- South Indian
- Chinese
- Continental
- Italian
- Multi-cuisine specialty dishes
- Soups, Starters, Kebabs & Curries
